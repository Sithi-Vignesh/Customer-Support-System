ABC Technologies SaaS Platform Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: How do I create an account?
A: Select the Sign Up option, provide your required information, verify your email address, and complete the account setup process.

Q2: I forgot my password. What should I do?
A: Use the "Forgot Password" option on the login page. A password reset link will be sent to your registered email address.

Q3: How often am I billed?
A: Customers may choose either monthly or annual billing. Charges are processed automatically at the beginning of each billing cycle.

Q4: Am I eligible for a refund?
A: Customers may request a refund within seven days of the original purchase, subject to the company's refund policy and applicable terms.

Q5: Can I upgrade my subscription plan?
A: Yes. You may upgrade your plan at any time through your account settings. New features become available after the upgrade is processed.

Q6: Can I downgrade my subscription?
A: Yes. Downgrades are available according to your billing cycle and may take effect at the start of the next renewal period.

Q7: Is there a limit on file uploads?
A: Yes. Upload limits depend on your subscription plan, available storage capacity, and any applicable file size restrictions.

Q8: Which web browsers are supported?
A: The platform supports the latest versions of Google Chrome, Microsoft Edge, Mozilla Firefox, and Safari for the best experience.

Q9: Does the platform provide API access?
A: Yes. API access is available on eligible subscription plans and allows secure integration with approved third-party applications.

Q10: Can multiple users share one account?
A: Individual login credentials should not be shared. Organizations can add multiple authorized users according to the user limit included with their subscription plan.

Q11: How do I invite additional users?
A: Administrators can invite team members through the user management section by entering their email addresses and assigning appropriate permissions.

Q12: Is my information stored securely?
A: Yes. Customer data is protected using industry-standard security measures, controlled access, and regular security monitoring.

Q13: What should I do if I experience login or application errors?
A: Verify your credentials, check your internet connection, clear your browser cache if necessary, and try again using a supported browser before contacting support.

Q14: Can I cancel my subscription?
A: Yes. Subscription cancellations can be requested according to the company's cancellation policy. Service remains active until the effective cancellation date.

Q15: How do I contact technical support?
A: Technical support is available through the customer support portal, email, and live chat. When requesting assistance, include your account information, a description of the issue, any error messages, and the troubleshooting steps already completed to help expedite resolution.
